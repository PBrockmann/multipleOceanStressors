Multiple stressors of ocean ecosystems

Here, we present an interactive interface to explore simulations published in Bopp et al. (2013) to assess how four major stressors of open ocean ecosystems could evolve over the course of the 21st century: sea surface warming, surface pH, subsurface dissolved O2 concentration, and primary productivity by marine phytoplankton (NPP).

Navigate to "Explore/Download" to view simulated changes in stressor intensities under each RCP scenario plotted on synchronized, zoomable maps or as a timeseries.
